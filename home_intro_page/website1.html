CHEMICAL ENGINEERING CLUB (ChEC)

What is ChEC?

ChEC is the official student-run chemical engineering club of IIT Tirupati. Our mission is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world engineering through interactive workshops, industry talks, student competitions, and research-driven initiatives.

We aim to foster innovation and critical thinking among students by engaging them in activities that promote technical growth, interdisciplinary collaboration, and sustainable solutions to real-world challenges.
