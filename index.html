Chemical Engineering Club (ChEC) IIT Tirupati

Inaugurated on 5th September 2024 to bring chemical engineering students together and help them grow academically.

"From the very beginning, the club has created a support system for first-year students so they can feel confident and enjoy learning chemical engineering."

Our Vision

  • To motivate students to genuinely enjoy and feel confident about chemical engineering.
  • To support them in pursuing their studies with enthusiasm and exploring career options.
  • To help them appreciate and choose core engineering over other disciplines.
  • To develop graduates who are skilled, confident, and valued in the professional world.

Our Aim

  • Organize regular technical lectures, seminars, and interactive academic events.
  • Facilitate mentorship programs and peer learning groups, especially for first years.
  • Provide exposure to diverse career pathways, internships, and networking with alumni.
  • Conduct industry and laboratory visits to bridge the gap between theory and practice.
  • Actively counter negative stereotypes about chemical engineering by highlighting success stories.

About Us & Leadership

Our club organizes technical talks, group study sessions, workshops, and project activities focused on making chemical engineering easier and more interesting for everyone.
